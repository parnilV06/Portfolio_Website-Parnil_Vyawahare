About me

Hi, I’m Parnil Vyawahare — a first-year B.Tech CSE student at MIT ADT x Kalvium, pursuing Software Product Engineering through a work-integrated, industry-focused curriculum. I’m an aspiring Full Stack Developer with a passion for building functional, user-friendly, and creative digital experiences.
I started my coding journey back in grade 8, and since then, I’ve been exploring and growing in the world of technology. Right now, I’m honing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C/C++, while also learning advanced tools, frameworks, and modern web development practices. My roadmap includes mastering React, Next.js, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, and expanding into languages like Python.
Alongside my studies, I work with Painganga Publication as a Web Developer & Manager, building and managing their websites on WordPress and Wix. I also enjoy experimenting with no-code platforms like Webflow and integrating them with custom code to create flexible, high-performance solutions.
For me, coding isn’t just about writing programs — it’s about turning ideas into reality, solving problems, and creating tools that make a difference. I’m always learning, always building, and always aiming to code up to the future 🚀..

Join me on my journey as I transform my passion for coding into reality, Taking one step at a time !
